## 📹 Screen Recording Instructions

1. Open `promo.html` in **Google Chrome**
2. Press **F11** for full screen (1920×1080)
3. Wait for fonts to load (2–3 seconds)
4. Start your screen recorder, then **refresh the page** (F5) — video starts auto-playing
5. Record for ~70 seconds
6. Stop recording after Scene 8 fades in

**Recommended screen recorders:**
- OBS Studio (free, best quality)
- Windows built-in: Win+G → Record
- Loom (easy, cloud)

---

## 🎬 Video Editing Tips

1. Add voiceover audio track (record yourself or use AI voice)
2. Recommended AI voice: **ElevenLabs** (Hindi/Hinglish voices available)
3. Trim the beginning (remove font load time)
4. Add subtle sound effects on each scene transition
5. Export as MP4, 1920×1080, 60fps
